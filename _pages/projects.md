InsideRadar:
---
InsideRadar is an insider trading detection platform on Polymarket. We (my friends Andrew, Angadh, and I) built an XGBoost model and a real-time data pipeline to flag bets likely influenced by insider information, using Polars, SQLAlchemy, FastAPI, Scikit-learn, and NetworkX. This work is in progress: more to come soon!

PolyBayes:
---
[PolyBayes](https://github.com/yshahzad/PolyBayes) is a tool for quantifying uncertainty and conviction in prediction markets. By modeling the market price as a stochastic process, it fits Dynamic Bayesian credible intervals to historic or live market trades. This offers novel insight into how certain the market is of an outcome, beyond the live trading price.


ProfSight McGill: 
---
[ProfSight](https://yshahzad.github.io/projects/class-avg) is an interactive data dashboard that aims to help undergraduates at McGill University make informed choices about their degree planning. It is built using Python (BeautifulSoup, Pandas, Plotly) and hosted on Streamlit.
